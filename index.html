About Our Association

The Senegalese Association of Minnesota serves as a vital hub for the Senegalese diaspora in the Twin Cities and surrounding areas. Founded to preserve our rich cultural heritage while facilitating integration into American society, we provide support, resources, and community connections for Senegalese immigrants and their families.

Our mission is to foster unity among Senegalese Americans, promote cultural exchange, and contribute positively to the broader Minnesota community through education, social services, and cultural preservation.

Our Values

  • Teranga: Hospitality and warmth
  • Jëf: Honor and dignity
  • Ndey Djirim: Community support
  • Xam-Xam: Knowledge and education

Our Community Services

Cultural Programs

Language classes, traditional dance, music workshops, and cultural celebrations that keep our heritage alive for future generations.

Integration Support

Assistance with immigration processes, job placement, housing resources, and navigating American systems.

Youth Development

Educational support, mentorship programs, and leadership development for Senegalese-American youth.

Social Services

Emergency assistance, family support, elder care, and connections to local resources and services.
